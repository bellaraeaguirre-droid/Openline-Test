Questions
Things people ask.
Does it show everything, including private browsing? ↓
Private/incognito browsing is not tracked by default. openline only logs standard internet activity. We built it this way intentionally so both partners feel genuinely comfortable opting in.
Can my partner see it without me knowing? ↓
No. Sharing requires both people to download the app, create accounts, and actively pair together. Your partner cannot activate this without your explicit participation.
What exactly does it show, apps or just websites? ↓
Both. openline shows which apps are using the internet (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, games, etc.) and which websites are being visited, along with how long and how often. It's a full picture of internet activity, not just browser history.
What if I want to stop sharing? ↓
You can pause sharing at any time from the app. Your partner will be notified that you've paused, but won't see why. There's no punishment, no lock-in.
Is this legal? ↓
Yes. Because both parties explicitly consent and both can see the same data, openline operates entirely within legal boundaries. It's fundamentally different from covert monitoring apps, which are illegal in most jurisdictions.
