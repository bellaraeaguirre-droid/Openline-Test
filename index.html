Questions
Things people ask.
What exactly does it show, apps or just websites? ↓
Both. breakup. shows which apps are actively using the internet (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, games, Discord, etc.) and which websites are being visited, along with how long and how often. It's a full picture, not just browser history.
Can my partner see it without me knowing? ↓
No. Sharing requires both people to download the same app, create accounts, and actively pair together. Your partner cannot activate this without your explicit participation.
Does it show private or incognito browsing? ↓
Private/incognito browsing is not tracked by default. We built it this way intentionally so both partners feel genuinely comfortable opting in, not pressured.
Does it drain my battery or slow my phone? ↓
breakup. runs as a lightweight background profile. Battery and performance impact is minimal, similar to any standard VPN or parental control profile.
What if I want to stop sharing? ↓
You can pause sharing anytime from the app with one tap. Your partner will be notified that you've paused, but won't see why. No punishment, no lock-in.
Is this legal? ↓
Yes. Because both parties explicitly consent and both see the same data, breakup. operates entirely within legal boundaries in all US states. It's fundamentally different from covert monitoring apps, which are illegal in most jurisdictions.
